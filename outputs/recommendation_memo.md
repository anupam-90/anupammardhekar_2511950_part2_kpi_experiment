# Recommendation Memo: Onboarding Campaign Analysis

## Executive Summary
This memorandum outlines the business problem and the evaluative framework for the A/B test conducted between the current onboarding experience (Control) and the new campaign-driven experience (Treatment).

### Problem Overview
We are tasked with deciding whether to implement the new onboarding campaign. The core goal is to boost conversion without triggering unintended negative consequences in customer support or refund activity.

### Success & Guardrail Framework
* **Primary Success Metric:** Conversion rate to paid plans.
* **Secondary Metric:** Onboarding completion rate and user engagement score.
* **Guardrails:** Support ticket volume and refund frequency.

### Strategic Requirement
The recommendation to launch the new campaign depends on demonstrating a positive lift in the conversion funnel while ensuring that the guardrail metrics (support tickets and refunds) do not show a statistically significant increase, which would signal poor quality of acquisition.

*Note: Data analysis is ongoing. Further sections of this report will detail the statistical significance of these results.*
